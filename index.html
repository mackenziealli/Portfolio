For COGS 120: Interaction Design, I worked on a team to create a mobile application in response to a need we discovered students had. The application was a centralized way of accessing course information: announcements, deadlines, grades, and course resources, while staying informed about their status and progress. We all contributed equally in the storyboarding and wireframing process. I and one teammate handled the development timeline and user testing. I personally contributed the most to the simplicity of the homepage and clarity of the icons.
